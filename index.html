Bio

God One is a Certified Hip Hop cultural specialist certified by the Temple of Hip Hop through the Master Teacha KRS-ONE. His live show consist of a non-stop powerblast of Beat-boxin, Rap and Deejayin. With around 40 show performance under his belt, he opened alongside LiftiD for Ra The Rugged Man in his 2024 Canadian Tour, performing 13 show across Canada and USA. He and LiFTiD also opened for Slick Rick in Toronto in 2024. He is also a fresh Deejay that has deejayed for 50+ rap artist and held it down for more than 20 events.
